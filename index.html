About Us

Dedicated to providing a conducive campus environment through excellence in facility management.

Our Mission

The Campus Facility Management Office is committed to the effective planning, development, operation, and maintenance of campus facilities, infrastructure, and support services. It ensures the provision of safe, accessible, efficient, and environmentally responsible learning and working environments through quality service, sound resource management, and continuous improvement.

Our Vision

A premier campus facility management office that provides safe, sustainable, resilient, and learner-centered environments that support academic excellence, research, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Role

The Physical Plant and Facilities office is dedicated to the holistic upkeep of the campus. From daily maintenance and utility management to large-scale infrastructure projects, we ensure operations run smoothly.

Our Goals

Key institutional objectives directing our physical plant and facilities management towards excellence.

01

Safe & Conducive Environments

Ensure that all campus buildings, classrooms, laboratories, offices, and open spaces are safe, functional, and conducive to teaching, learning, and research.

02

Efficient Facility Maintenance

Implement preventive and corrective maintenance programs to preserve the integrity and functionality of campus facilities and equipment.

03

Environmental Sustainability

Integrate sustainable practices in energy management, water conservation, waste management, and campus greening initiatives.

04

Campus Safety & Preparedness

Support the implementation of health, safety, security, and disaster risk reduction measures to protect campus stakeholders and assets.

05

Infrastructure Development

Plan, monitor, and facilitate infrastructure improvement and expansion projects aligned with institutional development goals.

06

Operational Efficiency

Optimize the utilization of financial, human, and material resources to ensure cost-effective facility operations and management.

07

Accessibility & Inclusivity

Promote accessible and inclusive facilities that accommodate the needs of all students, faculty, staff, and visitors.

08

Stakeholder Satisfaction

Deliver responsive, reliable, and quality facility services through active collaboration and continuous engagement with the campus community.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive solutions to maintain and improve the campus infrastructure, ensuring optimal functionality and safety.

Maintenance

Routine and preventive maintenance of campus buildings, grounds, electrical, and plumbing equipment to ensure longevity and usability.

Infrastructure Dev

Strategic planning, monitoring, and execution of new construction, continuous renovations, and significant campus improvements.

Safety & Security

Upholding rigorous safety standards, managing emergency preparedness protocols, and maintaining a highly secure campus environment.

Utilities Management

Continuous and efficient management of fundamental resources including power, water, internet connectivity, and waste disposal systems.

Campus Facilities

Classroom

Well-ventilated and highly equipped spaces designed for interactive and comfortable learning experiences.

Computer Laboratory

State-of-the-art technological facilities providing students with essential computing power and resources.

Administrative Office

Organized, welcoming hubs for vital student services and university personnel operations.

Common Area

Spacious, clean, and vibrant environments tailored for student socialization and academic collaboration.

Library

The academic resource center of the campus, storing students' theses, feasibility studies, and reference books to support research and academic inquiry.
